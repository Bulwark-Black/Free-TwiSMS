Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bulwark Black LLC and its Rural Tech and Support service ("we", "us", "our") collect, use, and protect your information when you contact us or use our phone and text messaging services.

Information we collect

We collect your name, phone number, and the content of the calls or messages you send us. You provide this when you call, text, or request service from us.

How we use your information

We use it to reply to your messages, send appointment confirmations and account notifications, deliver verification codes you request, and provide customer support. We do not sell your personal information.

Text messaging

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information about your text messaging opt-in and consent will not be shared with any third parties. We use your mobile number only to send messages related to the service you requested.

Opting out

You can stop text messages at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

Data retention and security

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our service and protect it using reasonable safeguards.
