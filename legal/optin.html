SMS Opt-In and Consent

Last updated: May 30, 2026

How customers opt in

Customers give express written or verbal consent to receive text messages from Bulwark Black LLC and its Rural Tech and Support service in one of these ways:

  • In person or by phone: when a customer calls us or requests service, we ask for their mobile number and tell them they will receive text messages about their account, appointments, and verification codes, that message and data rates may apply, that message frequency varies, and that they can reply STOP to opt out at any time. The customer agrees before we add their number.
  • By text: a customer may start a conversation by texting one of our business numbers first. We reply only to customers who contact us.

What customers agree to at opt-in

At the time of opt-in, customers are told: "By providing your number to Bulwark Black LLC, you agree to receive text messages about your account, appointments, and verification codes at the number provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. See our Privacy Policy and SMS Terms."

Message types

Appointment confirmations, account notifications, one-time verification codes, and replies to customer questions. We do not send marketing or promotional messages, and we do not message anyone who has not opted in.

Frequency, cost, and opting out

Message frequency varies based on customer activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

Consent is not shared

Consent is not a condition of any purchase. No mobile information or opt-in consent is shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy and SMS Terms and Conditions.
